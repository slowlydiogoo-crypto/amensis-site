# Amensis — Psychological Horror Game (Landing Page)

**Official website:** https://amensis.netlify.app/  
Amensis is a **first-person psychological horror game** featuring **instant camera mechanics**, **zoom/focus clues**, and **puzzle-driven exploration**.

---

## About the game
**Amensis** is a slow-burn horror experience focused on tension, atmosphere, and unreliable perception.  
Your instant camera is not a tool — it’s the game’s language: what you capture can reveal hidden layers, messages, and threats… but it can also invite attention.

## Features (game highlights)
- 📷 **Instant camera gameplay** to reveal hidden details
- 🔎 **Zoom & focus** to discover micro-clues and distortions
- 🧩 **Puzzles & exploration** driven by observation and interpretation
- 🎧 **Sound & silence** used as tension and warning
- 🏚️ **Claustrophobic spaces** with shifting details and dread
