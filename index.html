About

Amensis is a slow-burn psychological horror game focused on tension, atmosphere, and the uncomfortable feeling that something is wrong. The story stays mysterious on this page — players should discover the plots inside the game.

The Camera

Your instant camera is not a “gadget” — it’s the game’s main language. What you capture can expose hidden paths, messages, and threats… but it can also invite attention.

✓ Reveal hidden layers in photos
✓ Zoom into details to unlock progress
✓ Use framing to find symbols & patterns
✓ Stay alert: the camera can show what you wish you hadn’t seen
